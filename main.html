The Problem

Human beings, ever since its creation has never been alone on the earth. As we all know we share this planet with lots of different species of living creatures, such as animals, and plants. But even though we name ourselves the superior species due to our ability to think, make decisions and choose how we want to live our lives, we start to grow. This led to us somehow neglecting the fact that we are not alone in this planet. We start pushing other species out of the way, and we sometimes don’t realize that the impact felt by those other species has terrible and sometimes deadly consequences, and also we sometimes fail to understand that it can also cause harm to us.

Our objective

  • Developing a positive human-companion animal relationship for our street animals.
  • Campaigning to end the use of all animals in entertainment, sacrifice and captivity.
  • Educating, researching and litigating to end the use and abuse of animals in food and clothing.

How Can We Save Stray Animals

  • Adopt- By adopting you make a loving animal part of your family. Adopt animals rather than buying them, it may save a life.
  • Donate- Donate for animals to make a happy change in their life.
  • Volunteer- If you don’t have money to give, you may give your time and care for them, or just send them to an animal shelter.
  • Speak Up- Speak up for these animals even they emotions, difference is that they can’t express but we can.
  • Follow Veganism- Veganism is a philosophy and way of living which seeks to exclude—as far as is possible and practicable—all forms of exploitation of, and cruelty to, animals for food, clothing or any other purpose
